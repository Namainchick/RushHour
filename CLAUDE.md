# RushHour Hackathon Context

Repo purpose: AI BEAVERS x Mollie Founder Hackathon, June 6, 2026.

Idea: an AI-powered marketplace matching businesses with content creators.

One-liner: Airbnb meets LinkedIn for creator collaborations, optimized for successful partnerships, not browsing.

Customer problem: businesses waste time and budget manually finding creators on Instagram, TikTok, agencies, and directories.

Creator problem: creators struggle with outreach, acquisition, competition, and visibility.

This is not just another creator directory, influencer marketplace, or UGC platform.

Core innovation: AI predicts which business-creator partnerships are most likely to work.

AI is the product, not a chatbot.

AI agents analyze brands, creators, goals, audiences, content style, portfolio, and past collaborations.

Outputs: structured profiles, compatibility scores, and a curated top match list.

Positioning: existing platforms optimize discovery; we optimize successful collaborations.

MVP workflow:

1. Business enters website / brand info.
2. AI creates a structured brand profile.
3. Creator profiles are analyzed.
4. Matching engine scores compatibility.
5. Business sees top 5 recommended creators.
6. Business swipes or selects matches.

Do not prioritize messaging, advanced dashboards, complex settings, analytics, or extra features.

Support UGC creators, influencers, local on-site creators, and professional content creators.

Future trust layer: ranking, quality scoring, reputation, performance tracking, and refund protection.

Likely business model: transaction fee, premium matching, subscriptions, or enterprise accounts.

Most likely payer: businesses.

Hackathon goal: clear problem, AI-native product, market opportunity, working MVP, founder-level thinking.

Judges reward specific buyer pain, market insight, demo execution, meaningful AI, evidence, and pitch clarity.

Avoid vague "everyone" markets; choose a wedge such as restaurants, hotels, gyms, retail, or e-commerce.

Pitch must cover problem, customer, why now, solution, differentiation, evidence, and next step.
